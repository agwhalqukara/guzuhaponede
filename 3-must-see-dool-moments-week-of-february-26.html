3 Must-See DOOL Moments Week Of February 26

Days of Our Lives spoilers reveal there are three must-see moments for the week of February 26. Holly Jonas (Ashley Puzemis) wakes up but can’t remember what put her in a coma.

That’s bad news for Tate Black (Jamie Martin Mann), who returns to Salem at a halfway house. Meanwhile, Stefan DiMera (Brandon Barash) is ordered to finish off Harris Michaels (Steve Burton).

Holly Jonas Wakes Up

DOOL spoilers for the week of February 26 reveal that one must-see moment features Holly. That’s right, Holly finally wakes up from her coma! This is wonderful news for many people in Salem, including Nicole Walker-DiMera (Arianne Zucker) and EJ DiMera (Dan Feuerriegel).

However, after Holly wakes up, she wants to know what happened to her. Nicole and EJ are hesitant to tell Holly the whole truth. Knowing Nicole, she fears that the shock or trauma could put Holly right back into a coma.

DOOL Spoilers: Tate Black Returns

During the week of February 26, another must-see moment centers on Tate. Since Holly’s overdose, Tate went to jail, was beaten up, and then had to stay at a southern Illinois drug rehab. Tate is pretty angry over how his life has derailed, especially since he doesn’t do drugs, and tried to stop Holly. Tate also blames his parents, Brady Black (Eric Martsolf) and Theresa Donovan (Emily O’Brien).

Now that Holly has woken up, Tate will hope that she will clear him. If Holly doesn’t remember, Tate might still face a legal uphill battle. In the meantime, Tate will return to Salem and stay at a halfway house.

Harris Michaels’ Death Ordered

Days of Our Lives spoilers for the week of February 26 reveal that one must-see moment centers on Harris. Fans watched as Ava Vitali (Tamara Braun) poured her heart out to Harris. Later, Harris woke up to see Rafe Hernandez (Galen Gering).

While everyone is overjoyed that Harris has woken up, it will make Stefan nervous. Soon after, Clyde Weston (James Read) orders Stefan to get rid of Harris permanently.

Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Week of February 26

Other must-see moments include Marlena Evans (Deidre Hall) questioning Everett Lynch (Blake Berris). Plus, Steve Johnson (Stephen Nichols) makes an admission to Konstantin Melounis (John Kapelos). Also, Eli Grant (Lamon Archey) and Lani Price-Grant (Sal Stowers) come back to Salem in light of Paulina Price’s (Jackee Harry) health crisis.
