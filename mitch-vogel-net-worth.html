Mitch Vogel Net Worth

Mitch Vogel is an American actor born on January 17, 1956 in Alhambra, CA. He is best known for his role as Jamie Hunter, the Cartwright's adopted orphan, on the television series Bonanza. Vogel's performance on the show earned him two Emmy nominations and a Golden Globe nomination.
