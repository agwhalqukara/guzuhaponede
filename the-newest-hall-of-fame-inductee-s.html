The Newest Hall of Fame Inductee(s)

Now on the other hand, their first characters of the Sheepherders are some of the violent, bloodthirsty evil men to ever set foot in a ring. Unfortunately, those people are never going to be mentioned here and it's all about the Bushwhackers, who are another team going in for nothing more than nostalgia.
