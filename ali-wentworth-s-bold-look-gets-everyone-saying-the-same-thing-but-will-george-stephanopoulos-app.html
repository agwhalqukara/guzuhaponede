Ali Wentworth's bold look gets everyone saying the same thing but will George Stephanopoulos app

Ali Wentworth has an incredible sense of style and as the popular TV host continues to promote her documentary on Brooke Shields, she has been sharing some stellar ensembles.

However, when she took to social media during the week, it wasn't her gorgeous black dress that caught the attention of her fanbase, but it was instead her golden locks. Ali wore her stunning hair loose, allowing the long strands to fall down past her shoulders as she made an appearance on the Howard Stern Show on SiriusXM.

The mom-of-two didn't reference her look in her caption, instead sharing: "Great time on the @sternshow with @robertabooey1 and @rahsaansalaam taking #PrettyBaby."

One follower noted: "Like the long locks," while a second enthused: "I love your hair," and a third posted: "Hair!!" alongside a pair of heart emojis.

Despite earning the approval of fans, the star's husband, George Stephanopoulos, has yet to share his thoughts on Ali's beautiful hair, but we're sure he's privately voiced his support.

Other followers praised Ali and George for their new documentary, Pretty Baby, which examines the life of legendary actress and model Brooke Shields as she grew up in the public eye, with her first modeling contract when she was 11.

The film will see Brooke looking back over pivotal moments of her life and explore a society that sexualizes young women and features George and Ali as producers. If you need to know more about the project, then check out the trailer below!

Last week, George shared an exciting update on the project after being absent from Good Morning America for several days.
