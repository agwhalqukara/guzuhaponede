Zoe Chao Net Worth: How Much Does the Strangers Star Make?

Zoe Chao is an American actress and screenwriter who has been making waves in the entertainment industry with her roles in Strangers, The Afterparty, Love Life, and more. Zoe Chao’s Early Life and Education

Zoe Chao was born on September 19, 1985, in Providence, Rhode Island, to a Chinese father and an Irish-English mother. She grew up in a family of visual artists and developed an interest in acting and writing from a young age. She attended The Wheeler School and graduated in 2004. She then pursued her BA in art history from Brown University and her MFA from the graduate acting program at UCSD.

Zoe Chao’s Career and Achievements

Zoe Chao started her career in theatre, appearing in productions such as La Jolla Playhouse’s Sideways, Surf Report, and WoW Festivals’ Our Town, and Ensemble Theatre Company’s Amadeus. She also worked with directors such as Christopher Guest, Les Waters, Ping Chong, and Chris Ashley.

Zoe Chao made her television debut in 2011, guest-starring in The Protector. She then appeared in shows such as Hart of Dixie, The Comeback, Rizzoli & Isles, Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn, The OA, and Living with Yourself. She also created, wrote, and starred in her own web series, The God Particles, in 2014.

Zoe Chao’s breakthrough role came in 2017, when she played Isobel Song, a bisexual woman who rents out her spare room on Airbnb, in the comedy-drama series Strangers. The show was critically acclaimed and screened at the Sundance Film Festival. Zoe Chao received praise for her performance and was nominated for a Gotham Award for Breakthrough Series – Short Form.

Zoe Chao continued to impress audiences with her roles in movies such as Where’d You Go, Bernadette, Downhill, The High Note, I Used to Go Here, Long Weekend, and Somebody I Used to Know. She also starred as Sara Yang, the best friend of the protagonist, in the romantic comedy anthology series Love Life, which premiered on HBO Max in 2020. She also joined the cast of the Apple TV+ mystery comedy series The Afterparty, playing Zoe Zhu, a former child star and pop singer, in 2022.

Zoe Chao has also been involved in voice acting, lending her voice to the animated series Creature Commandos, created by James Gunn for the DC Universe, in 2023. She is set to play Jane Kuang, a Chinese-American journalist, in the upcoming movie If You Were the Last, based on a novel by Mike Chen.
