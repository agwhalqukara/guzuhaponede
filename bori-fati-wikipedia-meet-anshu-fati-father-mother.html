Bori Fati Wikipedia: Meet Ansu Father And Mother

Ansu Fati was born to his parents; his father Bori Fati and to his mother Lurdes Fati.

His father Bori is a formal footballer having played for lower leagues in Portugal back in the day.

Bori was born in Guinea-Bissau and was an immigrant to Portugal. After his football feats in Portugal, he moved to Marinaleda, Seville, Spain.

The father of Ansu worked as a driver for the mayor Juan Manuel Sánchez Gordillo which helped to settle in Spain.

That was where Ansu spent most of his childhood. He was six years old when he moved to Spain and started his football training.

Bori has said he is “a Sevillian” though he was born in Guinea-Bissau.
